PX SmartFlow+

PX SmartFlow+ is an intelligent technology-based Paraxylene (PX) distribution system innovation that integrates IoT sensors, AI-powered routing, and manual override features. This system enables adaptive optimal path selection to Jetty 1 or Jetty 3, based on actual pressure, temperature, and flowrate. With this flexibility, the system can reduce vessel queues, minimize demurrage risk, and maintain supply continuity for downstream processes like PTA.

AI-Powered Routing System

Automatic jetty route selection based on pressure, temperature, and demand.

Forecasting Module

Jetty load & shipping needs prediction based on end-of-month demand trends.

Smart Valve (C²SP)

Flexible & real-time PX diversion to Jetty 1 or Jetty 3.

PX Booster & Thermo-Control

Smart pumps maintain PX viscosity during transfer.

Predictive Safety System

AI alarms for high pressure, temperature anomalies, or overloading.

Blockchain Log

Immutable records for audit, transparency, and accountability.
